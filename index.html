About Me

I am David Huang, an Executive Assistant at Tennessee SCORE, where I provide administrative support to the organization's President/CEO and Senior Vice President of Strategy. Raised in Nashville, I joined SCORE in 2024 after earning both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Computer Science from the University of Tennessee-Knoxville, where I focused on cybersecurity and technology integration.

My background encompasses business and technical dimensions, granting me a unique perspective that has enabled me to acquire proficient tools and skills in both domains, all while identifying meaningful intersections between them. My passions lie within technology consultation, cybersecurity, software engineering, and the dynamic world of Salesforce.

Enthusiastic about harnessing my education in computer science, coupled with my adept leadership abilities and business acumen, to catalyze organizational growth and drive lasting success.
